# Mechanistic Interpretability of Sparse Autoencoders

Recent breakthroughs in sparse autoencoders (SAEs) have allowed us to decompose the activations of large language models into millions of sparse, interpretable features. 

This post analyzes how these features correlate with the geometric priors discussed in our previous research on NLA.
