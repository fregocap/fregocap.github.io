The observer
is the observed.

I am Fabio Capela, a theoretical physicist interested in the intersection of differential geometry, information theory, and large language models.

My work focuses on interpreting neural networks not as black-box function approximators, but as geometric objects whose training dynamics can be analyzed using the tools of modern physics.

Scientific Background

With a PhD in Theoretical Physics, I've spent over a decade applying mathematical rigor to complex systems. You can find my previous research contributions on arXiv.
