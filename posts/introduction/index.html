Introduction

Posted Jun 21, 2020

Hello everyone. I'm a data scientist with a background in theoretical physics and I'm passionate about applying my mathematical/programming skills to understand markets and create codes to increase my revenues at the end of the month. That is why I've created this blog: to share my experimentations in algo trading, get advices from you and increase my knowledge in finance and how to become financially independent. On a more personal level, I live in the beautiful country of Switzerland, but of Portuguese descendance; I speak 3 languages, I`m happily married and have a beautiful (unbiased opinion) daughter.

This blog is my first attempt to dig out some of the financial strategies that I do see in the literature. It is also a blog about my journey to financial independence, as I do belong to a movement called the FIRE movement, standing for “Financially Independent, Retire Early”. A movement, born in the USA, that considers that people could actually retire early and become financially independent if you are disciplined about sparing a big chunk of your revenue at the end of each month, invest it into the markets and once you reach an amount of money (which typically corresponds to 25 times what you would spend per year), you are then allowed to withdraw 4% of the amount that you have per year.
